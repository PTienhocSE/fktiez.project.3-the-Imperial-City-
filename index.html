KHAI DINH TOMB

The Tomb of Khai Dinh, officially Ung Mausoleum is a tomb built for Khai Dinh, it blend of Vietnamese with Western styles, Emperor of the Nguyen dynasty of Vietnam.

HUE CITADEL

"Citadel of Huế, including Imperial City, Purple Forbidden City, Royal Canal, Museum of Huế, National University, Lake of the Serene Heart" part of Complex of Huế Monuments

TAM GIANG
